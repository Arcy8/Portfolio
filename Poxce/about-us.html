ABOUT US

        At Poxce Cafe, we believe in the power of a great cup of coffee to bring people together. Nestled in the heart of the city, our cozy cafe is a place where friends, families , and colleagues can unwind, connect, and savor the simple pleasures of life.

Our Story

        Established in 2024, Poxce Cafe has been a beloved fixture in the community for over a few months. Our journey began with a passion for crafting exceptional coffee and creating a warm, inviting space for our guests. Over the years, we've grown into a cherished meeting spot for coffee enthusiasts and food lovers alike.
